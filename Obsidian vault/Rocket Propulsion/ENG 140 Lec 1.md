# Momentum
$$P_1=(m+ \Delta m)V = P(t)$$
$$P_2 = m(V + \Delta V)+\Delta m(V-u_e) = P(t+\Delta t)$$
$$\therefore \Delta P = m\Delta V-\Delta mU_e$$
$$\Sigma F = \frac{dP}{dt} = m \frac{dV}{dt}-U_e \frac{dm}{dt}$$
$$\Sigma F = 0 = m\frac{dV}{dt}+U_e\frac{dm}{dt}$$
$$\therefore \Delta V = U_e ln(\frac{m_o}{m_f})$$
